## Ingredients

- 200 g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 1 lemon, zest and juice
- 25 g parmesan, finely grated
- Salt and black pepper
- Chili flakes (optional)

## Steps

1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve 120 ml pasta water.
2. Warm olive oil in a pan over low heat. Add garlic and cook 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Add lemon zest and chili flakes. Stir for 30 seconds.
4. Toss in pasta with a splash of pasta water. Add lemon juice and parmesan.
5. Adjust with more pasta water, salt, and pepper. Serve immediately.

## Notes

- Add parsley for a fresher finish.
- Works well with a handful of arugula stirred in off heat.
